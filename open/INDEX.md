# Index of open problems

Every problem, conjecture, and question posed in this repository carries a permanent identifier MAIS-O$n$, assigned in order of arrival and never reused. Standalone problem expositions live in this folder; the rest are posed inside the [research agendas](../agendas/), and the tables below locate each one by its number within the linked agenda. Where an item has no title of its own, a short editorial descriptor is given *in italics*.

Every item below is stated as open as of July 2026. When one is resolved, its row will say so. Progress reports and corrections: [open an issue](https://github.com/lionellevine/MAIS/issues/new).
